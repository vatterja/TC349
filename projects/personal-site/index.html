Welcome to my Website!

James Vatter is a student at Michigan State University getting his degree in Media Arts and Technology. He is an accomplished web developer, working for Michigan State Senate for many years working on websites. He currently runs his own Internet and Computer consulting business here in Lansing, Michigan where he also lives.

He is passionate about web programming and design and has accomplished many things in this area. One of the biggest projects that James Vatter is most proud of is helping write the Online voting system for the 2004 Democratic Presidential Primary.

During his years working for the Democratics, James developed many of the online systems that they still use today. He helped develop campaign organization systems, research systems for Senate bills, and is currently working on a campaign finance online reporting program for candidates to use for upcoming elections.
